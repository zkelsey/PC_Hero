Processor Details

Consider the Processor the brain of the system.

It tells every other component what to do and when to do it. Used interchangably with the term CPU, it is responsible for all of the arithmetic and logic operation for the computer. The speed, core efficiency, power draw, heat generation, and core count are all important factors to consider when choosing a CPU and will determine how effectively it does its job.
